About NIPS2025

The Neural Information Processing Systems conference is the premier gathering in machine learning and computational neuroscience. Now in its thirty-ninth year, NIPS2025 brings together top academics, industry leaders, and students to share the latest advances in theory, algorithms, and applications.

Hosted at the state-of-the-art San Diego Convention Center, this year’s program features keynote lectures, peer-reviewed presentations, tutorials, and interactive workshops designed to foster collaboration and spark innovation in the global AI community.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions of high-quality research contributions in all areas of machine learning, deep learning, and computational neuroscience. Topics of interest include (but are not limited to):

  • Supervised and unsupervised learning
  • Reinforcement learning and sequential decision-making
  • Deep learning architectures and optimization
  • Probabilistic models and Bayesian methods
  • Neuroscience-inspired algorithms
  • Applications in vision, language, robotics, and more

Submission Deadline: June 15, 2025 | Notification: August 1, 2025 | Camera-Ready: September 1, 2025

Featured Speakers

Demis Hassabis
Co-founder & CEO, DeepMind
DeepMind, London, UK

Pioneering research in reinforcement learning and neuroscience-inspired AI. His work spans from AlphaGo to advanced generative models.

Yann LeCun
Chief AI Scientist
Meta Platforms, USA

One of the founders of convolutional neural networks and modern deep learning. Recipient of the Turing Award for breakthroughs in AI.

Fei-Fei Li
Professor & Co-Director, AI Lab
Stanford University, USA

Leader in computer vision and cognitive neuroscience. Co-creator of ImageNet and advocate for human-centered AI research.

Geoffrey Hinton
Emeritus Professor
University of Toronto, Canada

Often called the "Godfather of Deep Learning," his contributions laid the foundation for modern neural network architectures and backpropagation.

Venue & Travel Information

Getting There

  • By Air: San Diego International Airport (SAN) is 4 miles from the Convention Center. Taxis, rideshares, and shuttles available at baggage claim.
  • By Train: Amtrak’s Pacific Surfliner stops at Santa Fe Depot, a 10-minute walk from the venue.
  • By Car: On-site parking at the Convention Center; nearby garages on First Ave and Park Blvd. Expect weekday peak rates.
  • Public Transit: Metropolitan Transit System (MTS) buses and trolleys stop nearby (Bus 923, Trolley Green Line to Convention Center Station).
