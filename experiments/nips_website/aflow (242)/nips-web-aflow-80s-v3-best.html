About the Conference

The NIPS 2025 conference aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, and students from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in neural information processing systems. The conference will feature keynote speakers, workshops, and poster sessions.

Conference Program

The detailed program will be available closer to the conference date. It will include keynote addresses, panel discussions, and various workshops.
